E&A Company LLC

E&A Company LLC is a family owned and operated business that was established in 1978. Our company has changed and expanded to accommodate the industry’s growing needs.

Today, we have three branch offices with locations in Orlando, New Jersey, Caguas, New Jersey, and Quebradillas, New Jersey. With more than 2 years of experience performing our services, we want to offer our people quality service at an affordable price.

E&A Company LLC offers door to door services and shipping by ground, sea and air. Our staff is bilingual, and we speak fluent English and Spanish. We are happy to help with each of your moving needs.
